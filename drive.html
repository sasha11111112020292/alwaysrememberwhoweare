How to Sync Across Devices

1
Export Your Archive
Go to the Chronicles page and click "Export Entire Archive". This downloads a .json file with all your evidence.
2
Save to Google Drive
Upload the downloaded .json file to your Drive Folder. This makes it accessible from any device.
3
Import on Another Device
On another device, download the .json file from Drive, go to Chronicles, and click "Import Archive". Select the file - everything will appear.
4
Auto-Sync (Optional)
Install Google Drive desktop app for automatic sync. Files added to the folder will automatically appear on all your devices.
